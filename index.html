Instructions

You play as HOME. Your card is on the right (desktop) or at the bottom (mobile). The opponent (GUEST) is on the other side.

How to play

  1. Choose one stat on your HOME card
  2. The opponent’s stat in the same category is revealed.
  3. The higher value wins the round.
  4. After each round, the game clock decreases by the amount set in the Settings (default: 1 minute).
  5. When the game ends, the player with the most points wins.

New round, new cards: At the start of every round, both you and your opponent get a new player card.
– Your HOME card is random.
– The GUEST card is chosen based on the current score difference: if you’re ahead, you’ll face tougher opponents; if you’re behind, you’ll get easier ones.

Color legend

  • Green on HOME → you score
  • Green on GUEST → opponent scores
  • Yellow → tie (no points)
  • Red → loses the round

Scoring by stat

  • 1 point: FT%, GP, MIN
  • 3 points: 3PM, 3P%
  • 2 points: all others (PTS, REB, AST, STL, BLK, FG%, EFF, …)

Data: Player stats come from the most recently finished NBA regular season, sourced from nba.com/stats
